ALSTROEMERIA is a dynamic startup at the intersection of technology and innovation. Founded by a team of enthusiasts, our company aims to solve pressing problems of excessive energy consumption. Our team combines expertise in electronics, verification software development, and physics, allowing us to approach problems from multiple angles. As a startup, we value agility, creativity, and a hands-on approach to solving complex issues.

Who we are

On average, 30-40% of all energy consumed in the world is spent on heating various premises and residential buildings. At the same time, about 40% is wasted due to ineffective control and automation systems. This not only has a significant impact on the environment, but also increases the costs of consumers themselves

Statistics

Our focus is on creating advanced sensors and high-tech electronic modules that monitor temperature and pressure in real time. These solutions are tailored to reduce excessive energy consumption in heated environments, ensuring systems operate efficiently and sustainably. This way you will not only help nature, but also save your own money.
